Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Frank AI

Is Frank AI completely free? +
Frank AI is free to download and use. However, you'll need your own OpenRouter API key to access AI models. OpenRouter offers competitive pricing and pay-as-you-go options.
What platforms does Frank AI support? +
Currently, Frank AI is available for Windows, macOS, and Linux. We're working on optimizing the experience for each platform.
Is my data really private? +
Absolutely. All conversations and data are stored locally on your device. Frank AI doesn't collect, transmit, or store any of your information on external servers. Your privacy is paramount.
Do I need an internet connection? +
You need internet to connect to the OpenRouter API for AI responses. However, features like TTS, local storage, and the UI work completely offline.
Can I customize Frank AI's personality? +
Yes! Frank AI offers extensive personality customization. You can adjust response style, tone, verbosity, and behavior to match your preferences.
How does Frank AI compare to ChatGPT? +
Frank AI offers complete privacy, local storage, offline capabilities, and deep customization that web-based alternatives can't provide. It's designed for users who want control over their AI assistant.
Will there be a mobile version? +
A mobile companion app is planned for future releases. Stay tuned for updates on the roadmap!
How can I support development? +
You can support Frank AI through donations, spreading the word, providing feedback, and reporting bugs. Every bit helps make Frank AI better!
